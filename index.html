About the Project

The Intelligent Warning System is expertly designed to significantly enhance the safety of pedestrians and cyclists by integrating cutting-edge computer vision technologies, high-performance Axis cameras, sirens with built-in lighting, and advanced communication protocols like LTE and LoRa. This system efficiently detects approaching vehicles from areas with reduced visibility, triggering immediate and highly visible alerts to prevent accidents. Importantly, the system processes all data in real-time without storing images or video, ensuring privacy while delivering rapid responses. Beyond just offering protection, this platform serves as a powerful test bed for continuously analyzing and optimizing performance metrics, ensuring ongoing improvements in accuracy and efficiency. Ideal for smart cities, industrial zones, and any high-traffic environment, the Intelligent Warning System offers a scalable, future-proof solution for enhancing road safety and reducing risk.
